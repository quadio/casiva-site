What do we do

We help you reach more buyers and sell faster at a higher price.

1. Leverage Your Social Networks for Free

The more people that you reach the better chance of finding a buyer. That's why we built advanced social marketing tools and analytics for the consumer real estate market. It's perfect for the do-it-yourself crowd that doesn't mind the leg work. Discover what the market really thinks about your property for free.

2. Upgrade to the MLS + Lockbox

For more serious sellers, we offer a listing on the MLS. We make it easy to allow buying agents to show your place by shiping you a lockbox overnight. You'll get picked up by major public sites like Zillow, Redfin, and Trulia. Agents in your area can easily find your property and connect you with serious buyers.

3. Full Service

Too busy or confused to handle selling your home? Hire one of our energetic agents to help you manage selling your property. Sit back and relax while we take care of everything. We'll manage the marketing, hire contractors, connect with buyers, and even have coffee with you.
